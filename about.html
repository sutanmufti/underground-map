About

The London Underground Map is an online map created by Sutan Mufti (2024). It has the London Underground network plus Elizabeth Line on a map.

Background

I am a bit surprised that it is hard to find the London Underground geographical map. So, I made one. I hope this is useful for every one.

Attribution

The data on the map was obtained from OpenStreetMap. The basemap is from Maptiler. SVG assets were obtained from SVGrepo.

This webmap was made with SvelteKit, Maplibre, and Tailwind. Vector tile specification using Mapbox MVT statically produced with gdal/ogr2ogr.
